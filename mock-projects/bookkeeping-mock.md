## Tasks

### Part A: Manual (60 marks)

1. **Daybooks (15 marks)**
   - Complete Sales Day Book (with VAT analysis)
   - Complete Purchases Day Book
   - Complete Cash Book (bank columns)
   - Complete Petty Cash Book
   - Complete General Journal

2. **Post to Ledgers (15 marks)**
   - Sales account
   - Purchases account
   - Debtors accounts (ABC Café, The Tasty Bite)
   - Creditors accounts (Fruit Farm Ltd, Veg Supply Co)
   - VAT Control account
   - Bank account (from Cash Book)
   - Wages, Rent, Loan accounts

3. **Trial Balance (10 marks)**
   - Extract a trial balance as at 31 January 2026

4. **Bank Reconciliation (10 marks)**
   - Update Cash Book with bank charges
   - Prepare Bank Reconciliation Statement

5. **VAT Return (10 marks)**
   - Calculate VAT payable/refundable for Jan-Feb period

### Part B: Computerised (30 marks)

6. Set up GreenGrocer Ltd in your accounts package
7. Enter opening balances
8. Enter all transactions
9. Run trial balance — compare to manual
10. Run aged debtors/creditors reports
11. Generate VAT report
12. Backup data to external storage
13. Print trial balance and profit & loss account

### Part C: Reconciliation (10 marks)

14. Compare manual and computerised trial balances
15. Explain any differences found
