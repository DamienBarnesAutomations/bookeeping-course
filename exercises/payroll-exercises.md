## Exercise 2: Manual Payroll Calculation (Cumulative)

**Employee:** Sarah Murphy
**PPS:** 1234567S
**Weekly Gross:** €850
**Tax Credits:** €3,750 p.a. (€72.12/week)
**SRCOP:** €42,000 p.a. (€807.69/week)
**PRSI:** 4% (earnings above €352)
**USC:** 0.5% on first €12,012 p.a.

Calculate PAYE, PRSI, USC, Total Deductions, and Net Pay for **Weeks 1, 2, and 10**.

Calculate her pay for **Week 1** under emergency tax:

## Exercise 7: Legislative Change Impact

Compare take-home pay for an employee earning €35,000:

**Year 1:**
- SRCOP: €40,000, Personal Credit: €1,700, PAYE Credit: €1,700
- USC: 0.5% (€0-€12,012), 2% (€12,013-€22,920)
- PRSI: 4%

**Year 2:**
- SRCOP: €42,000, Personal Credit: €1,875, PAYE Credit: €1,875
- USC: 0.5% (€0-€13,000), 2% (€13,001-€25,760)
- PRSI: 4%

Calculate net pay for both years and explain the difference.
