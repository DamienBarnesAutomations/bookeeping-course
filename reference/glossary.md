# Glossary — Bookkeeping & Payroll

---

## Bookkeeping Terms

| Term | Definition |
|------|-----------|
| **Accounting Equation** | Assets = Liabilities + Capital. The foundation of double-entry bookkeeping. |
| **Accrual** | An expense incurred but not yet paid (or income earned but not yet received). |
| **Aged Debtors** | Report showing customer balances grouped by how long they've been outstanding (0-30, 31-60, 61-90 days). |
| **Aged Creditors** | Report showing supplier balances grouped by how long they've been owed. |
| **Assets** | Resources owned by the business (cash, equipment, buildings, debtors). |
| **Balance Sheet** | Financial statement showing assets, liabilities, and capital at a point in time. |
| **Bank Reconciliation** | Process of matching the cash book balance to the bank statement balance. |
| **Capital** | Owner's investment in the business (also called equity). |
| **Cash Book** | Book of first entry for all cash and bank transactions. |
| **Chart of Accounts** | Complete list of all accounts used in the nominal ledger. |
| **Credit Note** | Document reducing the amount owed (returns, refunds, allowances). |
| **Creditor** | Person/entity the business owes money to. |
| **Daybook** | Book of first entry where transactions are initially recorded. |
| **Debtor** | Person/entity that owes money to the business. |
| **Depreciation** | Spreading the cost of an asset over its useful life. |
| **Double Entry** | System where every transaction has a debit and a credit of equal value. |
| **Drawings** | Money or assets taken from the business by the owner for personal use. |
| **Folio** | Reference number used to cross-reference between books (e.g., SDB1 = Sales Day Book page 1). |
| **General Journal** | Book of first entry for non-regular transactions (corrections, adjustments). |
| **Imprest System** | Petty cash system where the float is restored to a fixed amount at each replenishment. |
| **Invoice** | Document requesting payment for goods or services supplied. |
| **Ledger** | Collection of accounts where transactions are posted from daybooks. |
| **Liabilities** | Debts owed by the business (creditors, loans, VAT). |
| **Nominal Ledger** | Main ledger containing all accounts (also called General Ledger). |
| **Petty Cash** | Small cash fund for minor expenses. |
| **Prepayment** | An expense paid in advance (e.g., insurance paid for the full year upfront). |
| **Profit & Loss Account** | Statement showing income and expenses over a period (also called Income Statement). |
| **Purchases Ledger** | Sub-ledger containing individual supplier accounts (Creditors Ledger). |
| **Sales Ledger** | Sub-ledger containing individual customer accounts (Debtors Ledger). |
| **Source Document** | Original document evidencing a transaction (invoice, receipt, bank statement). |
| **Suspense Account** | Temporary account used when the trial balance doesn't balance. |
| **Trial Balance** | List of all nominal ledger balances to verify debits = credits. |
| **Unlodged Lodgement** | Amount received and recorded in cash book but not yet on bank statement. |
| **Unpresented Cheque** | Cheque issued and recorded in cash book but not yet presented to the bank. |
| **VAT (Value Added Tax)** | Consumption tax on goods and services. |
| **VAT Control Account** | Nominal account tracking output VAT (payable) and input VAT (reclaimable). |

## Payroll Terms

| Term | Definition |
|------|-----------|
| **Cumulative System** | Tax calculated on total earnings from start of tax year to date. |
| **Emergency Tax** | Temporary high-rate tax applied when employee details are not available (40%, no credits). |
| **Employer PRSI** | Social insurance contribution paid by the employer (11.05% Class A). |
| **Gross Pay** | Total earnings before any deductions (basic + overtime + bonus + allowances). |
| **Net Pay** | Take-home pay after all deductions (Gross − PAYE − PRSI − USC − Other). |
| **P30** | Monthly employer return of PAYE/PRSI/USC to Revenue. |
| **P35** | Annual employer return summarising all payroll for the tax year. |
| **P45** | Certificate given to employee when leaving employment. |
| **P60** | Annual certificate showing total pay and deductions for the tax year. |
| **PAYE (Pay As You Earn)** | Income tax deducted at source from employee's wages. |
| **PRSI (Pay Related Social Insurance)** | Social insurance contribution (employee + employer). |
| **PPS Number (Personal Public Service Number)** | Unique identifier for each person in Ireland for tax and social welfare. |
| **RPN (Revenue Payroll Notification)** | Electronic notification from Revenue showing an employee's tax credits and cut-off point. |
| **ROS (Revenue Online Service)** | Online portal for submitting tax returns and payments. |
| **SRCOP (Standard Rate Cut-Off Point)** | Maximum income taxed at 20% before the 40% rate applies. |
| **Tax Credit** | Amount deducted from tax owed to reduce the tax payable. |
| **Tax Year** | 1 January to 31 December. |
| **USC (Universal Social Charge)** | A tax on gross income collected alongside PAYE (rates: 0.5% to 8%). |
| **Week 1/Month 1 Basis** | Tax calculated on each period in isolation (no cumulative carry-over). |
