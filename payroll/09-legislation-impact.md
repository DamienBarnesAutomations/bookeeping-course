# Learning Outcome 9 — Legislation Impact

**Objective:** Analyse the impact of changes in legislation on personal tax and take-home pay by comparing two tax years.

---

## How Legislation Changes Affect Payroll

Each year, the Irish government announces budget changes that affect:

- Tax rates and bands
- Tax credit amounts
- PRSI rates and thresholds
- USC bands and rates
- Statutory deductions (pension auto-enrolment, etc.)

## Analysing Budget Changes: 5-Step Process

1. **Identify changes** — What rates, credits, bands changed?
2. **Calculate old liability** — Using previous year's figures
3. **Calculate new liability** — Using current year's figures
4. **Compare difference** — How much does the employee gain/lose?
5. **Explain the impact** — Which specific change(s) caused the difference?

---

## Employer Impact

| Change | Employer Effect |
|--------|----------------|
| Minimum wage increase | Higher payroll cost |
| Employer PRSI rate change | Direct cost increase/decrease |
| Pension auto-enrolment | New administrative burden + contributions |
| Sick pay legislation | Cost of statutory sick pay |
| Parental leave changes | Additional leave costs |
